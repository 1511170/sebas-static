General

How does the connection with BingX/Bitunix work?

expand_more
Our platform uses read-only/execution API connections with military-grade encryption protocols. This means your funds always remain in your personal exchange account; Sebas.co only syncs strategic operations automatically, ensuring you keep full control over capital withdrawals at all times.

What is Sebas.co and who is it for?

expand_more
Sebas.co connects influence, capital and technology in crypto. We are strategic partners of BingX and Bitunix and serve KOLs and influencers, professional traders, investment funds, and platforms or communities. We provide institutional-grade access, revenue-sharing infrastructure, and strategy—not custody of funds.

What trading volume does Sebas.co handle?

expand_more
Our ecosystem moves between 2 and 4 billion US dollars in monthly trading volume. This scale gives us direct desk access and institutional conditions with BingX and Bitunix that are not available on their public sites.

Is the ecosystem safe for retail investors?

expand_more
Security is our core pillar. We implement periodic Smart Contract audits and dynamic risk filters that protect against extreme volatility. We also operate exclusively with regulated, market-leading (Tier 1) exchanges, ensuring the technical infrastructure always meets your expectations.

Do you custody or hold my funds?

expand_more
No. We never custody client funds. All capital stays in your own exchange accounts (BingX/Bitunix). We only connect via secure APIs to enable execution, copy-trading or automation—you retain full withdrawal control at all times.

How do I get started or request a call?

expand_more
Use the “Schedule a demo” or “Schedule strategic call” buttons across the site—they link to our Cal.com booking. You can also reach us on Telegram (@sebasCua) for a quick conversation. We’ll align your profile (KOL, trader, fund or platform) and next steps.

What are the implementation or setup fees?

expand_more
Many of our models are performance and revenue-sharing based. For complex tech integrations or white-label projects we may agree an initial setup fee depending on scope. Details are discussed during your onboarding call.

How long does full integration take?

expand_more
Standard exchange connections and copy-trading setup usually take 5–10 business days. Custom white-label or multi-venue integrations can take 4–6 weeks. We’ll give you a clear timeline after the diagnosis phase.

In which regions or countries do you operate?

expand_more
We work with clients globally. Our infrastructure and partners (BingX, Bitunix) serve multiple jurisdictions. Compliance and eligibility depend on your profile and location—we clarify this during the application or first call.

How are you different from a normal affiliate program?

expand_more
We are not a standard affiliate program. We are strategic partners of BingX and Bitunix with direct desk access, institutional fees, and products that don’t appear on their public site. We build revenue ecosystems (revenue share, APIs, white-label, strategy), not just referral links.

What are the requirements for KOLs?

expand_more
We look for opinion leaders who prioritize real value for their community. We don't require a minimum follower count, but a high level of engagement and a philosophy aligned with financial transparency. We offer custom tracking tools and a competitive revenue-share model based on volume managed.

How do KOLs earn with Sebas.co?

expand_more
KOLs earn through revenue sharing when their audience trades on BingX or Bitunix via their links or copy-trading. We provide exclusive monetization channels, tracking dashboards, and growth support so your audience becomes a recurring revenue stream—up to six figures monthly for top performers.

Do I need a minimum number of followers?

expand_more
We don’t set a fixed follower minimum. What matters is engagement, alignment with transparency, and willingness to use our infrastructure. We’ve worked with creators at different sizes; the right fit is decided in the evaluation call.

Can I offer copy-trading or signals to my community?

expand_more
Yes. We provide copy-trading and signal infrastructure so your community can follow your strategies or signals. You earn from the volume they generate, with full control over how you present it and our support for compliance and best practices.

Is there exclusivity or can I work with other exchanges?

expand_more
We focus on BingX and Bitunix as our core partners. Exclusivity terms, if any, depend on your tier and agreement. This is discussed during onboarding so you know exactly what you can and cannot do elsewhere.

What benefits do professional traders get?

expand_more
Institutional rebates and minimum fees, turnkey copy-trading infrastructure, high limits, 24/7 VIP support, and advanced risk management tools. We connect you to BingX and Bitunix with conditions reserved for institutional desks.

How does the trader scaling program work?

expand_more
The program is for traders who already generate meaningful volume (e.g. $10K+/mo) and want to scale to $100K+ without trading more. We provide institutional access, copy-trading setup so others follow you, rebates, and infrastructure—turning your edge into an asset class.

What are institutional rebates and fee tiers?

expand_more
We negotiate institutional fee tiers and rebates with BingX and Bitunix that are not available on retail. Exact levels depend on your volume and profile. After onboarding we assign your tier and you see the impact on your costs and rebates.

Can I run copy-trading or a signal service?

expand_more
Yes. We offer turnkey copy-trading and signal infrastructure. You trade; your followers replicate or follow your signals. You earn from their volume and we handle the tech and exchange connectivity so you focus on performance.

Is there a minimum volume to qualify?

expand_more
We look for serious professionals. There isn’t a single public minimum—it depends on your profile (retail pro vs. small fund). The idea is that you’re ready to scale and use our infrastructure. This is assessed in the application or call.

How does a fund scale within the ecosystem?

Scaling is automatic through our liquidity management algorithm. As your strategy demonstrates consistency and audited profitability, our network of partners and institutional funds injects capital to amplify returns, allowing you to go from managing thousands to millions in record time under a robust infrastructure.

What compliance or reporting do you offer for funds?

expand_more
We support international compliance structures and automated, transparent reporting. Funds get clear reporting on volume, fees and flows. Exact deliverables depend on jurisdiction and strategy—we align this in the diagnosis and integration phases.

Do you provide deep liquidity or multi-venue access?

Yes. Funds get access to deep liquidity through our partners (BingX, Bitunix) and our infrastructure. We can also work on multi-venue setups so you tap liquidity across venues in a controlled way.
